# Orbit B-Hyve Gen 2 Bluetooth Valve Devices

A comprehensive overview of the Generation 2 (Gen 2) ecosystem of Orbit B-hyve Bluetooth-capable hose faucet valve devices. This ecosystem features two primary design tracks: the standard screenless Gen 2 Smart Timer (app-centric) and the B-hyve XD Smart Timer series (hybrid devices featuring a built-in digital display screen).

Both variations rely on upgraded Bluetooth 4.2/Wi-Fi hardware that pairs with Orbit's Gen 2 Wi-Fi Hub (the black version) for extended range and smart watering features.

### Important Ecosystem Compatibility Note
Gen 2 B-hyve hose timers use an upgraded communication architecture designed specifically to pair with the **Gen 2 Wi-Fi Hub** (identifiable by its solid black plastic casing). They are generally not backward-compatible with the original Gen 1 gray Wi-Fi hubs. Conversely, Gen 2 valves can mesh together with other Gen 2 devices to dynamically boost your Bluetooth network footprint around your lawn.
